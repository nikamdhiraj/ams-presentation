Introduction

The Asset Management System (AMS) is an advanced, cloud-based platform designed to monitor, analyze, and optimize the performance of wind power assets across various regions.

It provides visibility into:

  • KPIs and Performance Metrics
  • Turbine Health Monitoring
  • Site Revenue Analysis
  • Environmental Benefits Tracking

Platform Modules for Wind Asset Monitoring

Comprehensive Wind Energy Management Structure

Overview

AMS Wind Performance is structured into six key modules:

  • Portfolio – High-level asset overview
  • State - Portfolio – Regional analysis
  • Site - Portfolio – Site-specific insights
  • WTG - Monitoring – Real-time turbine data
  • BD Analysis – Breakdown diagnostics
  • Reports – Data export and analytics

Each module delivers specific insights into the energy generation lifecycle, performance benchmarks, and reliability of wind turbine operations.

Portfolio

High-level View of Wind Energy Assets

Portfolio Focus

High-level view of wind energy assets with comprehensive KPIs:

  • WTG Nos | Total number of turbines
  • Installed Capacity | MW capacity installed
  • Generation | MWh generated in selected fiscal period
  • Revenue | Cr revenue from wind energy
  • CO₂ Saved | MT of avoided emissions
  • Power vs Wind Speed Graph | Turbine efficiency analysis
  • Map | Overall installed capacity across states
  • PLF% Table | Daily Plant Load Factor per turbine

State – Portfolio

State-wise Performance Breakdown

State Portfolio Focus

State-wise breakdown of wind energy performance:

  • KPIs per State | Generation, CO₂ Saved, Revenue
  • Map View | Color-coding by state performance
  • Regional Strengths | Top performing states
  • Underperformance Identification | Areas needing attention
  • Comparative Analysis | State-to-state comparison

Highlights regional strengths and underperformance areas for strategic decision-making and resource allocation optimization.

Site – Portfolio

Site-level Deep Dive Analysis

Site Portfolio Focus

Site-level deep dive into individual wind farm performance:

  • Individual Site Performance | Detailed site metrics
  • Energy Output | Site-specific generation data
  • Capacity Analysis | Installed vs utilized capacity
  • Revenue Tracking | Site-wise revenue generation
  • Site Comparison | Detect issues or improvements
  • Status Indicators | Running, maintenance, fault status

Enables detailed performance monitoring and comparison to optimize operations across multiple wind farm sites.

WTG – Monitoring

Turbine-level Real-time Monitoring

WTG Monitoring Focus

Turbine-level real-time monitoring capabilities:

  • Live Status | Real-time status of each WTG
  • Wind Data | Wind speed and direction data
  • Power Output | Real-time power output in kW
  • Alerts System | Faults, alarms, and alerts
  • Maintenance Logs | Maintenance logs and history
  • Performance Analytics | Individual turbine performance

Provides comprehensive real-time visibility into individual turbine operations for proactive maintenance and optimal performance.

BD Analysis

Failure and Downtime Diagnostics

BD Analysis Focus

Comprehensive failure and downtime diagnostic capabilities:

  • Downtime Logs | Categorized failure tracking
  • Frequency Charts | Failure frequency analysis
  • Duration Analysis | Downtime duration tracking
  • MTBF | Mean Time Between Failures
  • High-Risk Components | Component failure identification
  • Preventive Maintenance | Maintenance suggestions

Enables proactive maintenance planning and reduces unplanned downtime through predictive analytics and failure pattern analysis.

Reports

Data Export and Presentation Tools

Reports Focus

Comprehensive data export and presentation capabilities:

  • Custom Reports | Tailored report generation
  • Scheduled Reports | Automated report delivery
  • Generation Summaries | Power generation reports
  • Revenue Reports | Financial performance analysis
  • PLF Summaries | Plant Load Factor reports
  • Multi-Level Exports | State, site, or WTG level data
  • Multiple Formats | PDF, Excel, CSV exports
  • Automation Options | Email delivery scheduling
